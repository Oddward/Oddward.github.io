During my bachelor's, I was involved with 2 committees that carried out entrepreneural projects in tourism, taking care of the graphics production, marketing as well as acquiring and handling some customers.

Towards the end of my bachelor's, I did an internship for a period of 6 months in a software company called IIUM Advanced Technologies (IAT). There, I was exposed to an industry development environment, gained valuable experience in full stack development and helped deploy some deliverables on-site overseas.

Having studied in an international setting since childhood with a very diverse demographic I built up my written and verbal communication skills along with lots of presentations and reports during higher education. The colourful education helped develop an open mind and global as well as local thinking. The lead role of group projects was often granted to me and I like to think I made the most of it.

Having done foundation studies in Engineering and a Bachelor's degree in computer science, I was exposed more to various emerging topics such as blockchain, cybersecurity and wireless sensor networks, and built solid fundamentals in economics, entrepreneurship and business design. I always had a passion for art which gravitated towards digital art and the environment in which it thrives. From there, many software skills were self-taught including various graphic design and productivity software. Various projects and courses I've done further exposed me to more software including Solidworks for CAD and Wolfram Mathematica for mathematical programs.

Entering the world of highly interactive software further grew my interest in software and UX design and their impact, and the development of useful and progressive software applications.

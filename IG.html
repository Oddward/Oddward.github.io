Experience

During my bachelor's, I was involved with 2 committees that carried out entrepreneural projects in tourism, taking care of the graphics production, marketing as well as acquiring and handling some customers. It was very hands on with no assisstance from the university. Both trips were successful and taught us quite a bit.

I was a research assisstant in my department for a period of 3 months under a distinguished professor, doing research as well as coding and running small programs under the study of quantum optics in which I did my final year project.

Towards the end of my bachelor's, I did an internship for a period of 6 months in a software company called IIUM Advanced Technologies (IAT). There, I was exposed to an industry development environment, gained valuable experience in full stack development and helped deploy some deliverables on-site overseas. During the time working with them on the MES & Enterprise system I learned a lot including the use of bitbucket and online repositories with a team and development with the Laravel PHP framework, and was exposed to deployment at the local server of the client.
